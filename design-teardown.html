Person Of The Week

Michelle Barker (@MicheBarks) is the lead front-end developer at Atomic Smash, a WordPress and WooCommerce design and development studio based in Bristol. She describes herself as a “CSS superfan” and loves to tinker with new CSS features. To help others learn CSS, Michelle writes about CSS topics on her blog CSS { In Real Life } and shares her knowledge speaking at conferences and meetups. She is also a Mozilla Tech Speaker. Thank you for everything you do for the community, dear Michelle!
